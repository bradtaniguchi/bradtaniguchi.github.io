Weird and interesting places to remote work from?

Read on dev.to

Due to the increase in opportunities that provide remote work, I was wondering if anyone ever tried to take their remote work and work from somewhere "totally out there", or that would at least standout.

Sure it's safer to work from home, but sometimes it feels nice to go out and go somewhere different, at least to change things up.

I was recently at a local garden and thought about grabbing my laptop and using their free wifi and working from there.

It got me thinking if anyone has tried working remotely, pre-pandemic, or now, from somewhere you'd consider weird or interesting.
